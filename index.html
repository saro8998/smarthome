Smart Lighting

Control your lights from your phone or voice. Turn lights on automatically when you walk in, set schedules, or create scenes like Movie Night or Goodnight — all in one tap.

Home Security

Keep your home safe with smart sensors and alerts. Get notified instantly if a door opens, motion is detected, or something unusual happens — even when you're not home.
